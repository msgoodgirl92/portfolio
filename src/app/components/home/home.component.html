About Me

My story with the digital world started back in 2014, when I finished my studies in web design and instantly fell in love with creating beautiful and intuitive websites. That curiosity soon pushed me further into the world of PHP and JavaScript, where I discovered how much I enjoy bringing ideas to life through code....
